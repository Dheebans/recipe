Homemade Garlic Shrimp Pasta

account_circle Extracted from @ChefJules • 15 mins • 2 servings

check

250g Spaghetti or Linguine

12 Large Shrimp, peeled and deveined

Main

4 cloves Garlic, minced

1/2 cup heavy cream

Fresh Parsley and Lemon wedges

Instructions

1

Boil the Pasta

Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water according to package instructions until al dente.

2

Sauté Aromatics

While pasta cooks, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
