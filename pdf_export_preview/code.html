Artisan Tomato & Basil Penne

A simple, rustic classic with fresh garden herbs.

Ingredients

200g Penne

2 Cloves Garlic

Fresh Basil

Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Sea Salt

Cracked Black Pepper

Method

1

Bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a rolling boil. Cook the penne pasta until perfectly al dente, usually 1-2 minutes less than the box instructions.

2

While pasta cooks, thinly slice the garlic. Saut√© in extra virgin olive oil over medium-low heat until fragrant and just barely golden. Do not burn.

3

Reserve half a cup of pasta water. Toss pasta directly into the pan with oil and garlic. Add tomato puree and hand-torn basil. Emulsify with pasta water as needed.
